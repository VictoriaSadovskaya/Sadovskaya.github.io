{ \bf { Complex Numbers}} 

\vskip.7cm

{\large
$\C = \{\, a+bi:\; a,b\in \R\},\;$ 
\vskip.1cm
where $i$ is the imaginary unit,\, satisfying $\,i^2=-1$.

\vskip.5cm

There is a one-to-one correspondence between complex numbers 

and points on the $xy$-plane: \, $a+bi \; \longleftrightarrow \; (a,b)$
\vskip.6cm

Addition: $\; (a+bi)+(c+di)=(a+c)+(b+d)i$
\vskip.4cm

Multiplication:  $\;(a+bi)(c+di)=(ac-bd)+(ad+bc)i$

\vskip.6cm

Modulus or absolute value: $\; |a+bi|=\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\,=$ distance to 0

\vskip.3cm

For any $z, w\in \C$, \, $|zw|=|z|\, |w|$ \, and \, $|z^n|=|z|^n$



\vskip.4cm
The complex conjugate of $\,\,c=a+bi\,\,$ is $\,\,\bar c=a-bi, \,\,$ and $\,\,|\bar c|=|c|$.
\
