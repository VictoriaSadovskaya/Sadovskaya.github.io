Sierpinski Triangle
    At each step, we remove the open central triangle from each of the triangles.
    Sierpinski Triangle is obtained by this infinite process.
    It is self-similar: it consists of 3 copies of itself scaled by the factor of 1/2.

    Suppose that the area of the first triangle (Step 0) is 1.
    Then the area at step n is (3/4)n, which tends to 0.
    Hence the two-dimensional Lebesgue measure of the Sierpinski Triangle os 0.
