Koch Curve
    Construction: Start with an interval. Replace its middle third by the two sides
    of an equilateral triangle of the same length as the removed segment, as shown.
    Next, take each of the four resulting segments, and replace its the middle third
    by two sides of an equilateral triangle.   Continue.
    Koch Curve is self-similar: it consists of 4 copies of itself scaled be the factor of 1/3.

    It is continuous but nowhere differentiable.
