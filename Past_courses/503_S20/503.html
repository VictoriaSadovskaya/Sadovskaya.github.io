    Course description:  This course develops the theory needed to treat linear integral
    and differential equations, within the framework of infinite-dimensional linear algebra.
    Applications to some classical equations are presented.  The course covers the
    following topics: Banach and Hilbert spaces, dual spaces, linear operators, distributions,
    weak derivatives, Sobolev spaces, applications to linear differential equations.
    Prerequisite:  Math 501.

    Text:  Lecture Notes on Functional Analysis  by A. Bressan.
